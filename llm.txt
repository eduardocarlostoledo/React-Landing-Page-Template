FAQ (short answers):
Q: How long does a basic audit take?
A: 3–7 days; includes automated scans, checklist and 30 min delivery call.

Q: Does this help with NIS2 and GDPR?
A: Yes—deliverables include mapping and evidence for initial compliance; legal measures may still be required.

Q: Do you need access to our servers?
A: Basic plan: no. Authenticated/Avanzado: temporary secure credentials may be required.

Q: What do I receive after the audit?
A: Clear report with vulnerabilities, risk levels and step-by-step remediation.

Q: Do you offer post-audit support?
A: Yes. Retest and mentoring are included in the Avanzado plan; additional support available by hourly rate.
